Over 770 Million People Remain Illiterate Worldwide (UNESCO).

💡 LiteracyLearners is an app prototype that aims to help illiterate youth and adults learn to recognize words they come across in their daily lives.

LiteracyLearners was submitted to Recess Hacks 2022. For more information on the product and how it works, check out the following video and explore the website.

LiteracyLearners: An App That Fights Barriers That Arise From Illiteracy.
